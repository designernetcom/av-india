PU Sheet

Rubber is physiologically inert, thus making it the preferred choice of medical, pharmaceutical and food processing industries. Its resistance to oil and hydrocarbon products is fairly limited and similar to that of Chloroprene rubbers. Reasonable resistance to a whole range of general chemical products, but acids, alkalis, esters and kerosene should be avoided.
